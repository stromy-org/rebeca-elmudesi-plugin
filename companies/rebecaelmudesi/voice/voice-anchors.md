# Rebeca Elmúdesi Voice Anchors (L2)

Ground-truth reference passages, taken verbatim from the artist's own website
and input. Treat these as higher-priority than any generic "professional" or
"art-marketing" intuition. When a draft does not sound like the same writer as
these anchors, simplify until it does. Do not paraphrase these into livelier
prose — the restraint is the voice.

## Anchor — artist bio register (verbatim, rebeca-elmudesi-website)

> Rebeca Elmúdesi (Dominican Republic, 2003) is a visual artist based in Madrid
> whose pictorial practice explores the mechanisms of perception, the construction
> of identity, and the tensions that arise between one's own gaze and the gaze of
> others. Rooted in her Dominican background and shaped by a European formative
> experience, her work moves through expressive figuration, psychological spaces,
> and surreal disruptions, creating images that feel at once intimate and strangely
> exposed.
